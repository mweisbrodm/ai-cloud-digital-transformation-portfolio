# Predictive Analytics for Hotel Booking Cancellations

## Project Type
Machine Learning | Decision Systems | Business Strategy

## Executive Summary

This project used machine learning to analyze hotel booking data and predict which reservations were most likely to be canceled. The business objective was to help hotel operators reduce revenue loss, improve occupancy planning, and make more informed decisions about cancellation and refund policies.

The project was structured around a practical business challenge: cancellations and no-shows create lost revenue, increased distribution costs, and operational uncertainty. The analysis translated booking patterns into policy recommendations that could support both customer satisfaction and business performance.

## Business Problem

Hotels face revenue and operational risk when customers cancel reservations, especially close to the arrival date. Last-minute cancellations can leave rooms unsold, reduce profit margins, and require additional staff time to manage inventory and guest arrangements.

The goal was to use historical booking data to identify cancellation patterns and recommend strategies that improve revenue predictability.

## Approach

1. Defined the business problem and objective.
2. Reviewed booking data and customer attributes.
3. Conducted exploratory data analysis.
4. Prepared the data by addressing missing values, categorical variables, and class balance.
5. Built and compared machine learning models.
6. Selected a final model based on performance.
7. Translated findings into operational recommendations.

## Tools and Concepts

- Exploratory data analysis
- Decision tree models
- Random forest models
- Data preprocessing
- Train/test split
- Model comparison
- Business recommendation development
- Revenue and operations analysis

## Key Business Questions

- Which booking characteristics are associated with higher cancellation risk?
- How can hotels use prediction to improve planning?
- What policy changes could reduce revenue loss without damaging customer experience?
- How can data-informed decision-making improve operational reliability?

## Strategic Recommendations

- Adjust cancellation and refund policies based on risk factors.
- Monitor high-risk booking segments more closely.
- Use lead time and booking behavior to inform occupancy planning.
- Develop customer communication strategies that reduce preventable cancellations.
- Balance revenue protection with customer satisfaction.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Translating a business challenge into an analytics project
- Interpreting machine learning output for nontechnical stakeholders
- Connecting data insights to operational strategy
- Evaluating technology through a business lens
- Communicating recommendations clearly

## Executive Takeaway

Predictive analytics can help organizations move from reactive decision-making to proactive planning. The value of the model is not only technical accuracy; it is the ability to support better business policies, improve resource planning, and reduce avoidable losses.
